FOREST ARRESTS DATA ANALYSIS - SUMMARY REPORT
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Dataset: 1366 arrests from forest conservation area
Time period: 2023-2025 (primarily)

Key Findings:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Most common crime: Suspected Illegal grazing
   (335 cases)

2. Gender distribution: 98.5% Male

3. Top receiving station: Mtito
   (295 suspects)

4. Most affected county: Makueni (178 suspects)
